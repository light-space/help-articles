# AR Aging

Light tracks AR aging at the customer level, with a widget that breaks open balances into standard aging buckets.

## Where to Find AR Aging

The aging view in Light is the **customer aging widget** on each customer's overview page:

1. Go to [**Revenue & Invoicing → Customers**](https://app.light.inc/customers)
2. Open a customer
3. The **Overview** tab shows the aging widget

There is no separate organization-wide AR aging report page; aging information lives on each customer record and is reflected in the overall AR data accessible through the standard reporting tools.

## Aging Buckets

The widget categorises open invoices into:

| Bucket | Days past due |
|---|---|
| **Current** | Not yet due |
| **1–30 days** | 1–30 days overdue |
| **31–60 days** | 31–60 days overdue |
| **61–90 days** | 61–90 days overdue |
| **91+ days** | More than 90 days overdue |

## Drilling into a Bucket

Click any bucket in the widget to open the Sales invoices list pre-filtered to that aging range and that customer.
