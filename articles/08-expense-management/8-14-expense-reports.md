# Expense Reports

In Light, an expense report is a batch of expenses an employee submits together for reimbursement. This article explains how expense reports work and where to review and analyze expense data.

[Open in Light →](https://app.light.inc/expenses)

## Understanding Expense Reports

An expense report groups one or more expenses into a single reimbursement submission:

- Employees add expenses (with receipts) on the **Expenses** page
- Submitting sends all open expenses for review as one reimbursement
- Each reimbursement tracks the expenses it contains and its payment status

Light does not use standalone expense reports for analytics — reporting on spend happens through the ledger reports described below.

## Submitting an Expense Report

1. Navigate to **Expenses**
2. Review your draft expenses and make sure each has a receipt and the correct details
3. Click **Submit** to send your expenses for review

Once submitted, the expenses move through approval and payment as a reimbursement. See [Reimbursement workflow](/articles/08-expense-management/8-8-reimbursement-workflow.md) for details.

## Tracking Submitted Reports

- **Employees**: Navigate to **Expenses** to see the status of your expenses and reimbursements
- **Finance teams**: Navigate to **Reimbursements** to review submitted reimbursements across the company

## Analyzing Expense Spend

Reimbursed expenses post to your general ledger, so expense spend appears in your financial reports:

1. Navigate to **Reports**
2. Use the system reports (Balance Sheet, Cash Flow Statement, General Ledger, Income Statement, Trial Balance, VAT) to review posted expense activity
3. Use pivot table reports to slice spend by dimensions such as account or entity

See [Reporting overview](/articles/10-reporting/10-1-reporting-overview.md) for the full list of report types.

## Exporting Expense Data

Reports and transaction views in Light support CSV export for further analysis in spreadsheets or other systems. See [Exporting reports](/articles/10-reporting/10-11-exporting-reports.md).
