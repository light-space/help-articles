# Notification Preferences

This article explains how notifications work in Light and how to configure integrations for receiving alerts.

[Open in Light →](https://app.light.inc/settings/integrations)

## Understanding notifications in Light

Light notifies you about important events related to your work, such as tasks requiring your attention, approval requests, and updates to documents you're involved with. Notifications can be delivered through multiple channels: web app push notifications, mobile push notifications, and Slack messages.

## Web app notifications

You can configure push notifications within the Light web app to stay updated on events that involve you. To manage your web app notification preferences:

1. Go to [**Settings (gear icon) → Profile**](https://app.light.inc/settings/profile)
2. In the **Notifications** section, click **Web app**
3. In the dialog that opens, configure your preferences:
   - **Bill approvals**: Toggle notifications for approval requests, reminders, and payment batch approvals
   - **Card transactions**: Toggle notifications for authorizations and declines
   - **Invoice approvals**: Toggle notifications for invoice approval requests
   - **Reimbursements**: Toggle notifications for reimbursement updates (rejections, setup required, status changes)
   - **Tasks**: Toggle notifications for task updates and rejected requests
4. Click **Save** to apply your changes

## Mobile notifications

You can configure push notifications for the Light mobile app to receive alerts on your phone. To manage your mobile notification preferences:

1. Go to [**Settings (gear icon) → Profile**](https://app.light.inc/settings/profile)
2. In the **Notifications** section, click **Mobile**
3. In the dialog that opens, configure your preferences for each category:
   - **Bill approvals**: Toggle notifications for approval requests, reminders, and payment batch approvals
   - **Card transactions**: Toggle notifications for authorizations and declines
   - **Reimbursements**: Toggle notifications for reimbursement updates (rejections, setup required, status changes)
   - **Tasks**: Toggle notifications for task updates and rejected requests
4. Click **Save** to apply your changes

> Good to know: Mobile notification preferences are separate from web app and Slack. You can enable different categories for each channel based on how you prefer to receive alerts.

## Slack notifications

You can configure which Light notifications are sent to Slack, allowing you to receive alerts in your team's Slack workspace. To manage your Slack notification preferences:

1. Go to [**Settings (gear icon) → Profile**](https://app.light.inc/settings/profile)
2. In the **Notifications** section, click **Slack**
3. In the dialog that opens, configure your preferences for each category:
   - **Bill approvals**: Toggle notifications for approval requests, reminders, and payment batch approvals
   - **Card transactions**: Toggle notifications for authorizations and declines
   - **Reimbursements**: Toggle notifications for reimbursement updates (rejections, setup required, status changes)
   - **Tasks**: Toggle notifications for task updates and rejected requests
4. Click **Save** to apply your changes

> Good to know: Slack notification preferences control which events Light sends to Slack. Your organization must connect the Slack integration (see [Slack integration](#slack-integration-setup) below) before Slack notifications can be delivered.

## Slack integration setup

Light integrates with Slack to deliver notifications to your team's Slack workspace. To configure the Slack integration:

1. Go to [**Settings (gear icon) → Integrations**](https://app.light.inc/settings/integrations)
2. Find the **Slack** integration
3. Follow the setup instructions to connect your Slack workspace
4. Once connected, Light can send notifications to designated Slack channels based on each user's Slack notification preferences

> Good to know: The [Integrations](https://app.light.inc/settings/integrations) page also includes connections for **HubSpot** and **Banks**.

## Profile settings

Your profile contains personal settings that affect how you interact with Light:

1. Go to [**Settings (gear icon) → Profile**](https://app.light.inc/settings/profile)
2. The profile page includes tabs for:
   - **Contact Information**: Your personal contact details
   - **Notifications**: Configure your notification preferences for Web app, Mobile, and Slack channels (see sections above)
   - **User settings**: General user preferences
   - **Reimbursement details**: Your reimbursement configuration
   - **Organization details**: Information about your organization

## Best practices

- Configure notification preferences separately for each channel (Web app, Mobile, Slack) based on how you prefer to stay informed
- Enable different categories per channel—for example, you might want urgent card declines on mobile but routine approvals only in Slack
- Connect the Slack integration at the organization level to enable Slack notifications for all users
- Review your notification preferences periodically to ensure you're receiving alerts that matter without notification overload
- Keep your profile contact information up to date so Light can reach you when needed
