# Light Help Center — Article Hierarchy

> Article index for the Light help center, covering both admin and employee audiences.

---

## Role Guide

**Admin articles** (sections 1–14) are typically for users with role **admin**, **controller**, **AR clerk**, or **AP clerk**.

**Employee articles** (sections E1–E6) are typically for users with role **approver**, **reimbursement**, **cardholder**, or **requester**.
